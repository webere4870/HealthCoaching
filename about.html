What is Health Coaching?

Health coaching is a process that involves partnering with clients to help them make positive changes to their health and wellbeing. It is a client-centered approach that focuses on helping individuals set and achieve their health goals by providing support, guidance, and accountability.

Sessions

A health coach works with clients to develop a personalized plan of action that includes lifestyle changes, such as diet and exercise, stress management, and self-care. Health coaches also help clients identify obstacles to their health goals and provide strategies to overcome them. They may also provide education on various health topics, such as nutrition and exercise, and help clients develop skills and strategies to maintain long-term health and wellness.

The process

First, you purchase a scheduled package. Then, we setup regular meeting times to discuss your health goals, mindset, and plan of action. Finally, we re-evaluate during the last session and draw up long-term goals for success.

Meet

Depending on the package you have chosen, we will meet at regularly established intervals

Practice

You are given the opportunity to apply the things talked about during sessions and receive feedback over the duration of our sessions.

Change

Once we have met several times, the process is far from over! It is up to you to carry the things learned into your daily life.
